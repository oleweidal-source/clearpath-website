Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026. We are committed to protecting your personal data and complying with GDPR.

01

Introduction

Tutto Solutions ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website tuttoai.tech or interact with our services.

We are based in Norway and comply with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

02

Information We Collect

We only collect personal data when it is voluntarily provided by you in connection with contacting us or requesting our services.

Personal data we may collect

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business information

Automatically collected data

  • Analytics data (e.g., website usage statistics)

We do not collect

  • Cookies for marketing purposes
  • AI input data from users
  • Call recordings
  • Chat logs
03

How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to inquiries and communicate with you
  • To provide and deliver our services
  • To manage client relationships
  • To improve our website and services through analytics

We do not use personal data to train AI models.

04

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process personal data based on:

  • Consent
  • Legitimate interest
  • Contractual necessity
05

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers to deliver our services:

  • Stripe
  • OpenAI
  • n8n
  • GoHighLevel
  • Google Analytics
  • Hostinger
06

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred outside the EU/EEA, including to the United States, with appropriate safeguards in place.

07

Data Retention

We retain personal data for up to 6 months unless required otherwise by law.

08

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time
09

Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and access control to protect your personal data.

10

AI and Automation

We only process data to deliver services and do not train AI models on personal data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
