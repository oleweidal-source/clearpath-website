How We Work

A clear path from
idea to impact.

01
Discovery & Strategy
Week 1–2
We start by deeply understanding your business, competitive landscape, and target audience. Together we define exactly what success looks like and map the clearest route to get there — before a single pixel is drawn.
  • Brand & competitor audit
  • Target audience mapping
  • Scope & success metrics
  • Technology stack recommendation
02
Design & Prototype
Week 3–4
Our designers craft a bespoke visual identity and fully interactive prototype. You see and feel exactly what you're getting before a single line of code is written — revisions included until it's perfect.
  • Visual identity & colour system
  • Wireframes & interactive prototype
  • Mobile-first responsive layouts
  • Design review & sign-off
03
Build & Integrate
Week 5–8
We build your solution with performance, SEO, and long-term scalability in mind. AI and automation integrations are wired cleanly into your existing workflows — zero disruption, immediate impact.
  • Production-grade development
  • CMS & content setup
  • AI & automation integration
  • Performance optimisation
04
Test & Refine
Week 9
Thorough quality assurance across all devices, browsers, and real-user scenarios. We stress-test every interaction and refine until every detail is exactly right — nothing ships until it's excellent.
  • Cross-browser & device testing
  • Accessibility audit (WCAG 2.1)
  • Load speed & Core Web Vitals
  • Client acceptance testing
05
Launch & Grow
Week 10+
We handle the launch end-to-end and stay on as long-term partners. As your business evolves we optimise, expand, and adapt your digital presence — you focus on growth, we handle the technology.
  • Managed deployment & DNS
  • Analytics & conversion tracking
  • Ongoing support & maintenance
  • Quarterly performance reviews
